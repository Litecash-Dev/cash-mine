PAYMENTS

Payments for new blocks will occur after 10 confirmations. Litecash will allow transactions to stand for 12 hours, then they revert to the sender. The payment system will send again upon failure, but it is advisable to open your wallet once every 12 hours or so. Due to the lengthy confirmation times of custodial exchange wallets, it is not recommended that custodial exchange addresses be used. When you are mining to a running wallet and the pool's hash rate is high, some payments may occur rapidly. It is recommended to add the Litecash node at gibs.cash:10127 to your wallet's peer list or set it as the remote node to improve payment response and avoid transaction delays.
